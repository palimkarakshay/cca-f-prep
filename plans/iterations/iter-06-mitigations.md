## N43 — Cumulative-load reality check + scope cuts

- **8 linkable assets → 2 linkable assets.** Saves 24 hours.

  Saves 8 hours; full suite at v1.1.

~270 hours. Revised launch-blocking checklist: **~340–490 hours
(28–41 weeks at 12 hr/wk = 6–10 months)**.

*honest* — and the explicit 6–10 month timeline is now matched to
the reality of the operator's hours.
